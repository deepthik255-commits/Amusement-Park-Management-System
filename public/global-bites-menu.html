World Flavors: Sides, Desserts & Drinks

Explore global flavors with our curated selection of side dishes, desserts, and drinks from around the world!

🍟 Side Dishes

Truffle Fries (France)
Crispy fries drizzled with truffle oil and topped with Parmesan.
Kimchi (Korea)
Fermented spicy cabbage with garlic, ginger, and chili peppers.
Grilled Asparagus (Italy)
Asparagus spears drizzled with olive oil and balsamic glaze, grilled to perfection.
Couscous Salad (Morocco)
Light, fluffy couscous tossed with chopped vegetables, fresh herbs, and lemon dressing.
Sweet Potato Fries (USA)
Crispy sweet potato fries served with a side of spicy mayo dip.

🍮 Desserts

Baklava (Turkey)
Layers of flaky phyllo dough, filled with chopped nuts and honey syrup.
Tiramisu (Italy)
A classic dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone, and cocoa powder.
Churros with Chocolate Sauce (Mexico)
Fried dough sticks rolled in cinnamon sugar, served with warm chocolate dipping sauce.
Mango Sticky Rice (Thailand)
Sweet coconut sticky rice topped with fresh mango slices.
Pavlova (Australia)
Meringue base topped with whipped cream and fresh seasonal fruits like berries and kiwi.

🥤 Drinks

Sangria (Spain)
A refreshing wine-based cocktail with fruits like oranges, lemons, and berries.
Lassi (India)
A yogurt-based drink available in sweet or salty flavors, sometimes flavored with mango.
Pisco Sour (Peru)
A tangy, citrusy cocktail made with Pisco, lime juice, simple syrup, and egg whites.
Bubble Tea (Taiwan)
Sweet tea-based drink with chewy tapioca pearls, available in various fruit and tea flavors.
Mojito (Cuba)
A minty cocktail made with rum, lime juice, fresh mint leaves, and soda water.
